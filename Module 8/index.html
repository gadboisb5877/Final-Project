Super Smash Bros.

Smash Brothers is a game series that brings people together. There is no if, ands, or buts about that. Because of how well it brings people together people came to fall in love with this game. With good reason, this is one of the most enjoyable game series out there. Great, notable characters, enjoyable scenaries, and fun game modes. Then to toss in great game mechanics, you have everything need for a competitive community to build up.

Competitive Smash has started out with the first iteration called Smash 64. Ever since then, it has gone through many hurdles trying to get as big as it is now. Smash 4 is the first one in which the creators, Nintendo, is actually batting an eye to the competitive community.
