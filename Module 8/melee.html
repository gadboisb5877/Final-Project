Melee

Melee, without a shadow of a doubt, is one of the biggest sources of controversy in the fighting game community. That aside, Melee is a game with an enormous community ever after it fifteenth year of being released. Such a popular game has stood the test of time and no better way to show off the test than with their own documentary they did halfway through their lifespan.
